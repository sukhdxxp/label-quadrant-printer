# Product

## Register

product

## Users

A single owner-operator (personal use). Someone who occasionally needs to print
an image onto one quadrant of an A4 TownStix 4-per-sheet label layout and wants
it done quickly and correctly. They arrive with a file and a goal, not a desire
to learn software. Sessions are short and infrequent; the tool should be
re-learnable at a glance after weeks away.

## Product Purpose

Place an image into a chosen quadrant of an A4 4-up label sheet, preview it
exactly as it will print, and export a print-ready PDF. Fully client-side, no
backend, no accounts. Success is a single clean output: upload, place, export,
print at 100% scale, and the label lands where the preview said it would.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, unobtrusive. Three words: clean, exact, effortless. The feel is
an Apple system utility: light, spacious, neutral, quietly confident. It guides
without nagging and never shows off. The interface is a means to a printed
sheet, not a destination.

## Anti-references

- Heavy or enterprise chrome: dense toolbars, ribbon menus, cluttered Office
  style panels, settings sprawl.
- Skeuomorphism: fake paper textures, realistic drop-shadowed label mockups,
  decorative "physical" metaphors. The preview is an honest technical
  representation, not a photo.
- Generic SaaS dressing: gradient accents, hero-metric panels, identical card
  grids. None of it belongs in a focused single-task tool.

## Design Principles

- **Zero friction to first print.** The shortest credible path from file to PDF
  wins. Every step, control, and word must justify the delay it adds.
- **The preview is the contract.** What is shown on screen is pixel-for-pixel
  what prints. Geometry has one source of truth; never let the preview and the
  output drift.
- **Sensible defaults over configuration.** Good defaults (2 mm margin, contain
  fit, next free quadrant) mean most prints need no decisions. Configuration is
  available, never required.
- **Honest precision.** Accurate to the millimeter, with calibration when trust
  must be earned. Never fake accuracy with visual polish.
- **Get out of the way.** A tool used briefly and rarely should be instantly
  legible and leave no residue: no onboarding tax, no upsell, no clutter.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Lightweight by intent (personal tool). Maintain legible contrast on text and
controls, keep the wizard fully keyboard navigable, and respect
`prefers-reduced-motion`. No heavy investment beyond these baseline courtesies
unless needs change.
